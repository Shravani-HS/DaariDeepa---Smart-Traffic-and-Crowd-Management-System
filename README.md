# 🛕 Daarideepa – Smart Traffic & Crowd Management System

🚀 AI-powered platform designed to manage **traffic congestion and crowd density near religious places** using real-time data, predictive analytics, and intelligent routing.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

Religious places often face:
- 🚦 Heavy traffic congestion
- 🧍 Overcrowding during peak hours & festivals
- 🚑 Delays in emergency response
- ❌ Lack of real-time monitoring & prediction systems

Traditional systems are reactive, not predictive.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

Daarideepa is a **smart, AI-driven system** that:
- Predicts **crowd density in real time**
- Visualizes traffic using **heatmaps**
- Suggests **optimized routes**
- Recommends **best entry gates**
- Forecasts **future traffic & crowd trends**
- Provides a **voice assistant interface**

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🔴 1. Real-Time Traffic Heatmaps
- Visual representation of traffic congestion
- Color-coded zones:
  - Red → High Traffic  
  - Yellow → Moderate  
  - Green → Low  

---

### 🤖 2. AI-Based Crowd Prediction
- Predicts crowd density inside temple areas
- Uses historical + real-time data
- Detects peak time automatically

---

### 📊 3. Future Forecasting
- Predicts crowd & traffic for:
  - Next 15 minutes  
  - 30 minutes  
  - 1 hour  
- Helps in proactive planning

---

### 🚪 4. Gate-Level Intelligence
- Shows crowd at each entry point
- Suggests best gate with minimum wait time
- AI camera-based estimation with confidence levels

---

### 🗺️ 5. Smart Route Optimization
- Suggests fastest routes using:
  - Real-time traffic
  - AI analysis
  - Dijkstra algorithm

---

### 🎙️ 6. Voice Assistant
- Ask queries like:
  - “What is the current crowd level?”
  - “Which route is fastest?”
- Instant AI responses

---

### 🎮 7. Gamification
- Users earn:
  - ⭐ Points
  - 🔥 Streaks
- Encourages smart route selection
